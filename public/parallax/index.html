Reforestation: Why not?

By changing our meat consumption habits, it is possible to reduce the soil consumption.
It is also possible to use that land for reforestation to capture CO2.
For example, about 5 tons of CO2 could be captured yearly per hectare!

How much CO2 could each country save?

The given values refer to the amount of kg CO2 equivalents each meat producing country could save per capita.

Conclusion.

Eating only poultry might not be a realisable model because of different food cultures and lifestyles around the world. But it is at least a good way to question what can be done the reduce our climatic impact through changing our meat consumption habits.
